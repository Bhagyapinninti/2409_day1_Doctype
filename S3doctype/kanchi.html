Ut, dolorem a.Tamil Nadu has many famous temples that are dedicated to Lord Shiva. One of them is the Ekambareswarar Temple, located in Kanchipuram. It is famous as one of the Pancha Bhoota Sthalas and signifies the element Earth. visit the temple to worship Lord Shiva, as Ekambareswarar or Ekambaranathar, in the form of a Lingam and an idol Prithvi Lingam. Legend has it that Goddess Parvati used to worship Lord Shiva in the form of Prithvi Lingam (Shiva Lingam made of sand) at this place, under a mango tree. The lord, impressed by this gesture, materialized in person and married the Goddess.
